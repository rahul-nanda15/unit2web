Goal 4.1

WHAT IS TARGET 4.1

Free Primary and Secondary Education




"By 2030, ensure that all girls and boys complete free, equitable and quality primary and secondary education leading to relevant and effective learning outcomes."

Target 4.1 focusses on ensuring that children have access to free education in primary and secondary schooling and are enabled to successfully compete its education. This goal is indicated by the amount of students that reach minimum proficiencies in both literature and math. Also, it is important that the UN recognizes the known and unknown places highly concentrated with children without education to help ensure their completion of 4.1. Next, It is also important to know why this is an issue around the world.

WHY IS THIS A PROBLEM?

The only way to overcome this prolonging issue in our world today is to eliminate the factors that prevent children from accessing free primary and secondary education as stated in Goal 4.1.1. To do this we must ask ourselves Why is this a Problem or What Obstacles are in the children's way and make it hard for them and their families to send them to school.
